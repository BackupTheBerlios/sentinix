SENTINIX NEED MIRRORS

sentinix.1.beta01.iso was downloaded exactly 70 times the first 48 hours after my announcement (between 26th and 28th of October, 2003). My 64MB RAM, software RAID-5 FTP server (kaosphere.ath.cx, running Slackware and pure-ftpd) started to block incoming connections (it only accepts 25 simultaneous logins) and during the first day people had to fight their way in.

Want to provide a mirror? That would be very appreciated! I would appreciate FTP access so that I personally can upload new releases. Download through anonymous FTP would be best since it will consume much less resources than Apache or IIS. Only mirroring of the ISO image is needed, it's about 200MB and there would be 2 releases (maximum) present on the site (400MB worth of total space).
