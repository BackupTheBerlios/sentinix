DONATION

We have a PayPal account up where we gratefully accept any donation, whether just a dollar or more. We don't request anyone to pay anything - SENTINIX is non-profit and will live and breath without money. But, the project does nevertheless have expenses to cover; hardware for testing, CD-R/CD-RW discs, domain name renewal, Internet connection - well you know how it is. Also, many hours have been put into and is being put into the making and maintenance of SENTINIX. However, we mean that no-one should be obligated or feel pressured to pay anything, donation is completely optional. The people/companies who donate money to SENTINIX will be mentioned under the SPONSORS section of this web site. If you wish to be anonymous, just put that statement into the "Optional Comment" field during donation.

Please note: The money goes directly to me, the Project Manager of SENTINIX (Michel Blomgren, sentinix at kaosphere.ath.cx). I may distribute the money to others in the project or spend it myself on something the project is in need of. I personally guarantee that every cent will be used entirely for SENTINIX.
