THE DEVELOPERS

This is the current list of the people who help to develop this distribution. You may notice that I have changed things around a bit. I have decided to only put people in The SENTINIX Development Team section that actually are, on a continuous basis, developing SENTINIX. Because I am not only the project manager, but also the (extremely) busy lead developer of SENTINIX, I am unable to keep in contact with people, instead I ask of people interested in helping out with the project to keep in contact with me. Those who do keep in contact with me (on a regular basis) are, most likely, those who end up in the Development Team section below. Everyone else end up in the CONTRIBUTORS section below. All who contribute development/administration efforts to SENTINIX, no matter how small, will be listed under the CONTRIBUTORS section.
