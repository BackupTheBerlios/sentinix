The SENTINIX Salamander has now been decided that it will have to go. It reminds too much of Mozilla's gecko lizard and SuSE's cameleon. SENTINIX has to have a more unique logo than that! This logo may freely be used and modified as long as it's used to refer to and/or redistribute the official, unmodified SENTINIX GNU/Linux distribution.
